## Course Overview

BIOL 1108L is the laboratory component for the second semester of introductory biology for science majors. I was the instructor of record for this course. I developed lectures and course materials, delivered lectures to my classes of 25–30 students, and led them on fieldwork expeditions as part of their research projects. I was awarded the Outstanding Teaching Assistant Award in recognition of my work in BIOL 1108L.
